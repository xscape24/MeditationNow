My Journey into Meditation

Hi, I’m Eben! Meditation became a part of my life during a time when I was battling OCD and anxiety. I needed a way to regain control of my thoughts and emotions, and that’s when I found the Waking Up app by Sam Harris. Starting with just 10 minutes of mindfulness meditation before work each day, I discovered the transformative power of stillness and awareness.

Over time, this practice worked wonders for my mental well-being, helping me navigate life with a newfound clarity and calm. Now, I’m passionate about sharing this gift with others through Meditation Now.

What Inspires Me

My inspiration comes from a deep desire to help others find peace amidst the chaos of modern life. I believe that meditation is more than just a practice—it’s a journey toward understanding yourself and the world around you.

  • Favorite App: Waking Up with Sam Harris
  • Daily Routine: 10 minutes of mindfulness before starting the day
  • Biggest Lesson: Consistency and patience are the keys to transformation.
