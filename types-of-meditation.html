Types of Meditation

Explore different types of meditation to find the one that suits your needs.

1. Loving Kindness Meditation

Loving Kindness Meditation (also known as Metta Bhavana) is a practice focused on cultivating compassion and love for oneself and others.

2. Mindfulness Meditation

Mindfulness meditation involves paying attention to your thoughts and feelings in the present moment, without judgment or attachment.

3. Guided Meditation

Guided meditation involves listening to a teacher or guide who leads you through a process designed to help you relax or explore a certain topic.

4. Open Awareness Meditation

Open awareness meditation encourages you to observe your thoughts and feelings without focusing on any one thing, allowing your mind to wander freely.

5. Transcendental Meditation

Transcendental Meditation involves repeating a mantra to help the practitioner transcend their normal state of consciousness and enter a deep state of rest.
